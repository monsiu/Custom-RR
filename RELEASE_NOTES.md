## Custom RR v1.3.2

This release removes the old Play beta onboarding flow and updates app and
project messaging for Google Play production access.

### Changed

- **Play beta onboarding retired.** The home beta invite and beta tester
  thank-you cards have been removed from the app.
- **About page updated for production.** About now shows Google Play production
  status and links directly to the Play listing.
- **Production-access messaging cleanup.** README and changelog wording now
  reflect production access and first-release rollout status instead of beta
  opt-in instructions.
