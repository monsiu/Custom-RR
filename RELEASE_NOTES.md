## Custom RR v1.3.1

A small follow-up to v1.3.0 that makes the community ROM browser easier to find
and smooths out joining the Google Play beta.

### Changed

- **Community ROMs are easier to find.** The OpenDesktop community builds browser
  is now called "Community ROMs" and sits right under Custom ROMs in the menu,
  with a shortcut to it at the bottom of the Custom ROMs screen. It stays clearly
  marked as unvetted, third-party builds, separate from the curated catalog.
- **Smoother Play beta sign-up.** The Join the Play beta page now has a QR code
  and copy / share buttons so you can invite others in one tap, plus a clear
  reminder that staying installed for about two weeks is what lets Custom RR
  reach the public Play Store.
- **Play beta extras.** Beta testers see a short thank-you on the home screen, a
  new Beta testers section on the About page (names shown after launch), and a
  note that testers get new builds about five days before the other channels.
  Important fixes still go out to every channel at once.
