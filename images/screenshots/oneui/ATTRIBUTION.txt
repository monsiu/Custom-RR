One UI screenshots bundled with Custom RR
=========================================

These images show stock Samsung One UI and are used to illustrate One UI
firmware ports in the catalog (UN1CA, ArtisanROM). They were sourced from
Wikimedia Commons, downscaled to 1080px wide and re-encoded as WebP.
